About Me

Hi, I'm John Mudryk, a passionate Junior Developer with a love for coding that began at an early age. I first discovered programming through block coding on Scratch and quickly realized how much I enjoyed creating and problem-solving. My journey continued in high school, where I took several introductory programming classes and a computer science course that deepened my understanding of software development.

After high school, I began exploring more advanced programming by helping friends develop content for the video game Garry's Mod. This experience, which involved setting up servers and creating mods, taught me the practical side of coding and fueled my enthusiasm for game development and software engineering.

Currently, I'm honing my skills through the We Can Code It bootcamp, where I’m completing modules in software development. I've earned multiple smaller certifications, and I’m excited to keep building my expertise by taking on new projects that challenge me to grow as a developer.

In my free time, I enjoy continuing development for the GMod server I help with and immersing myself in fantasy stories. My goal is to become a full-stack developer who can build scalable, efficient web applications, and eventually transition into game development. I'm currently expanding my knowledge in front-end development to round out my skill set.
